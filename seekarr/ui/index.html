Tips

Recommended, but not required.
01
Sonarr/Radarr: Stop immediate failed-download retries
Radarr/Sonarr -> Settings -> Download Clients -> uncheck Redownload Failed
Why: failed releases can cause Radarr/Sonarr to search again immediately. If that happens repeatedly, it can create noisy retry bursts and make your indexers work harder than they need to.
Letting Seekarr handle retry timing keeps failed-download searches coordinated with your scheduled runs, rate caps, retry windows, and per-run limits.
02
SEERR: Add monitored items without searching right away
SEERR -> Services -> edit Radarr/Sonarr -> uncheck Automatic Search
Why: SEERR typically runs all of its searches at once. If someone requests a lot of shows or movies, that can send dozens or hundreds of indexer searches in a short burst.
Disabling Automatic Search lets SEERR add the items to Radarr/Sonarr as monitored, then Seekarr handles the searching at a paced interval using your configured limits.
